Hamid Reza Ghaeini

Biography

I am doing a bi-nationally supervised PhD in the pillar of Information Systems Technology and Design at Singapore University of Technology and Design and System Security Lab at Technische Universität Darmstadt. I'm working under guidance of Prof. Jianying Zhou, Asst. Prof. Nils Ole Tippenhauer, and Prof. Ahmad-Reza Sadeghi.
